Introduction -
The Hon. Supreme Court of India in Writ Petition (Civil) No.1082/
2020, passed judgment dt.26.02.2026, emphasizing that overcrowding in
prisons is a systemic issue and recognized Open Correctional Institutions (OCIs)
as a humane and cost-effective solution. To ensure institutional accountability
and effective implementation of the court's directives, the Hon. the Supreme Court
has mandated the constitution of a "Monitoring Committee" in every State and
Union Territory. In accordance with these directions, the State Government has
decided to constitute the State Level Monitoring Committee for the management
and expansion of OCIs in Maharashtra.
Government Resolution:-
The Government of Maharashtra hereby constitutes the 'State level'.
Monitoring Committee for the Management of OCIs as follows:
