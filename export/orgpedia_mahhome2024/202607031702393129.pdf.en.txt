Government Circular: -
The date of provisional seniority of Sub-Regional Transport Officer, Group-A Category in the Transport Department as on 09.01.2024 has been notified vide Circular No.2 dated 26.09.2024. Now, a provisional seniority card dated 01.01.2025 has been prepared consisting of officers of the Sub-Regional Transport Officer cadre serving from 01.01.2024 to 39.12.2024. This seniority is appended to this circular as "Annexure-A."
The provisional seniority is being released subject to the following pending court cases as well as other matters mentioned below: -
1) Ma. Against the order of the Bombay High Court in petition No. 2797/2015 dated 04/08/2017 regarding reservation in promotions, the State Government has issued a notice to Ms. Special Leave Petition (SLP) No. 28306/2017 filed and pending in the Supreme Court.
2) The Assistant Regional Transport Officer cadre, headed by the Additional Chief Secretary (Services), has been constituted to check the earlier date of appointment.

Government Decision No: MVD-0726 / Q. No.249 / Par-4
According to the report of the committee, the process of examining the cases of human dates and reviewing the service seniority suggestions is underway. Accordingly, the process of revising the service seniority lists of the Assistant Regional Transport Officer category published earlier is being carried out separately, and the seniority of the said category will also be published separately in case of change in the seniority of the Sub-Regional Transport Officer / Regional Transport Officer rank of the officers who are due on human date.
The Transport Commissioner, State of Maharashtra, Mumbai is requested to bring this seniority to the notice of all concerned officers who are working, retired or have gone on deputation from or left the establishment of the department included in the provisional seniority list accompanying this circular.
In case of any objection regarding this seniority, submit your written representation to the Department of Transport (Executive Branch-4, Mantralaya, Mumbai) through the Transport Commissioner within 15 days from the date of this circular. Submissions received thereafter will not be considered.
The government circular has been made available on the Government of Maharashtra's Www.maharashtra.gov.in website with the code 202607031702393129. The circular is being digitally signed and authenticated.
