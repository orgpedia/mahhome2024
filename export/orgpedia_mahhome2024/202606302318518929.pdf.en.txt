3. The officer transferred shall ensure that he / she joins the place of transfer within the period of his / her assumption of office as provided in the Maharashtra Civil Services (Period of assumption, non-acceptance of service and Provisions for suspension, dismissal and removal from service) Rules, 1981. The Commissioner, State Excise shall not grant leave to the officer concerned without the prior approval of the Government for any reason. Also, as per Rule 30 (c) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1981, no applications of an officer transferred by the original office shall be entertained.
If the officer concerned attempts to directly or indirectly press for a change in the transfer order, disciplinary action will be taken against the officer concerned as the act violates Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, treating it as misconduct.
